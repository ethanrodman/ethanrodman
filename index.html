Introduction

Hello friends and family! I have the incredible opportunity of working with Awakening Collegiate Fellowship in Milledgeville, as a Worship Coordinator and Men's Ministry Coordinator. This position will be during the 2026-2027 school year, focusing on ministry with the colleges in the city of Milledgeville, Georgia, and more specifically, Georgia College & State University.

Experience @ ACF

Before moving to Milledgeville, I wasn't seeing much personal growth in my life. I was doing poorly with my schoolwork, my relationships were strained, and I was struggling with mild depression and anxiety. During this time, my twin brother, Aiden, decided to move to Milledgeville to attend Georgia Military College. Here, he was introduced to Awakening, formerly known as the Wesley Foundation. He immediately plugged himself into this ministry, found incredible community, and began to grow in his relationship with the Lord in big ways. It was such an awesome thing for me to see, yet I was struggling at home, partially because he wasn't there, but also because I wanted to experience what he was experiencing in Milledgeville. The summer after his first semester, I signed a lease, packed my bags, and moved into a house with him and some of his friends. Throughout my time in Milledgeville, I have been attending Georgia Highlands College online. And even though I never went to any of the schools in Milledgeville, the students in this ministry have accepted me as one of their own. Here at ACF, my relationship with the Lord has grown exponentially. I have found freedom from depression and anxiety. I have found lifelong friends who push me to be a better man, striving to be more like Jesus every day.

During my time at ACF, I knew the Lord was calling me to join leadership! In my first year, I joined the prayer team, following the Lord's leading in wanting me to have a deeper prayer life. I also joined the worship team this year, although I wasn't too involved with the team this year. The next two years, I served on the worship team as an intern, playing both bass and acoustic guitar.

Ministry Areas

01

Worship

I have the honor of co-leading the worship team this year! Over the last few years of being involved here, this ministry area has been such a formative experience for me.

As a leader of the worship team, I will be charged with helping our congregation enter into a space where they are free to worship the One who is worthy of all our praise and worship. I am also excited to build a culture of unity among the worship team, where we set aside any attempts to be praised for our God-given skills and talents and instead worship the One who gave them to us. I have a deep passion for worship and am super excited to continue learning what it looks like to worship and lead.

02

Men's Ministry

Another area in the ministry that I will be leading is the Men's ministry. Over the past several years, the men's ministry here at Georgia College has been growing in such a special way. I am thrilled to continue the hard work that the men, and truly the women as well, have put into this ministry.

One focal point of this ministry that I will be helping to facilitate is a small group called Men's House. Men's House is a Bible study, open to any guy, that meets at the local Waffle House at 6:30 in the morning every Thursday. The heart behind this meeting is to create a space where men can practice discipline, accountability, confession, and learn what it means to be a man of God.

03

Discipleship

Discipleship is a cornerstone of our overall ministry. Over my time at Awakening, I have been a part of discipleship by both getting discipled by a staff member and discipling younger students in the ministry. For me, these have looked like one-on-one meetings, chatting about life and how the Lord is moving and working in our lives. These are opportunities to be vulnerable and get deep with one another.

This past year, during my own discipleship, my discipler has helped walk me through some pretty tough situations, encouraged me, and sometimes given me some tough love as I need it, but always pointed me to scripture and to what the Lord has to say about my circumstances. I'm looking forward to continuing my discipleship journey this year!

Ministry Services

Fresh

Fresh is our freshmen service we host at our building downtown. A typical Fresh service begins with a short game and then worship, a message, and small groups to end the night. At the beginning of the year, freshman students have the opportunity to sign up for small groups. In these small groups, they form friendships and dive deeper into each other's lives and the messages on Monday nights.

Awakening Wednesdays

Wednesday nights are our congregational worship nights. We meet at Magnolia Ballroom, a prominent building in Milledgeville and a part of the Georgia College campus. A typical Wednesday night starts with pre-service prayer an hour before the main service begins. This is a space for staff, interns, and leaders to come and pray for our college campuses and our city. Between the end of prayer and the main service beginning, as students gather into our meeting space, there is a chance for one another to let go of the stress from class, catch up with friends, and meet new ones. The service begins with announcements, and then we enter into our first set of worship. Then we hear a message from the teaching team, which is followed by more worship and ministry opportunities with the prayer team.

Coffeehouse

Coffeehouse happens every Tuesday and Thursday from 11-2, also at our downtown space. This is a space for students to come and have fellowship with one another, study, and enjoy free gourmet drinks made by the staff.
